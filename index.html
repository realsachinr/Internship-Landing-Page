Overview

This intensive program offers hands-on experience and practical skills in web development. Designed for students and beginners, it includes learning modules, hands-on projects, mentorship, and a final presentation. Join us to kickstart your career in web development!

Program Overview : -

  • • Duration: 1 Month
  • • Focus : Web development
  • • Target Audience: Students and beginners in web development
  • • Goals: To provide practical experience, enhance coding skills, and prepare interns for entry-level positions in web development.

Key Components: -

    • • Introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • • Fundamentals of responsive web design
    • • Introduction to popular frameworks like Bootstrap and Bulma

    Hands-on Projects: -

    • • Interns will work on real-world projects under the guidance of experienced mentors.

Benefits of the frontend internship

  1. Real-world experience: Gain hands-on experience working on real projects, enhancing your skills in frontend development.
  2. Mentorship: Receive guidance and support from experienced professionals, accelerating your learning curve and helping you grow as a developer.
  3. Practical application of knowledge: Apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, honing your problem-solving abilities and understanding of industry best practices.
  4. Networking opportunities: Connect with industry professionals and peers, expanding your professional network and potential career opportunities.
  5. Resume enhancement: Add valuable experience at a reputable company to your resume, making you more competitive in the job market upon completion of the internship.

Qualifications: Desired skills and technologies

  • • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • • Experience with frontend frameworks like React.js.
  • • Responsive design principles
  • • Version control with Git/GitHub
  • • Basic understanding of backend technologies (e.g., Node.js, MongoDB)
  • • Strong problem-solving skills
  • • Creativity and attention to detail
  • • Continuous learning mindset
